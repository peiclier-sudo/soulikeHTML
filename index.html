Soulike 3D Prototype (Third Person)

This is now a true 3D scene with a third-person follow camera and a mage hero.

  • Move: ZQSD (or WASD)
  • Jump: Space
  • Dash: Shift
  • Basic attack: Left click (throws a fireball toward camera direction)
  • Charged attack: Hold left click, release to throw a massive fireball
  • Camera orbit: Right mouse drag (camera only, no attack)
  • Mouse lock: Click canvas to lock/hide cursor (Esc to release)
  • Dash bind: Press B, then press any key or mouse button (including side buttons)
  • View mode: V (toggle Classic / Fortnite shoulder camera)
