Soulike 3D Prototype (Third Person)

Fast, stylish third-person combat with a dark-arcane hero. Focus: great play feel.

  • Move: ZQSD (or WASD)
  • Jump: Space
  • Dash: Shift (snappy burst + momentum)
  • Basic attack: Left click (quick arcane bolt)
  • Charged attack: Hold left click, release for a heavy blast
  • Camera orbit: Right mouse drag (camera only, no attack)
  • Menu in fight scene: Press M to return to dashboard
  • Mouse lock: Click canvas to lock/hide cursor (Esc to release)
  • Dash bind: Press B, then press any key or mouse button (including side buttons)
  • View mode: V (toggle Classic / Fortnite shoulder camera)
