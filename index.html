Soulike 3D Prototype (Third Person)

This is now a true 3D scene with a third-person follow camera.

  • Move: ZQSD (or WASD)
  • Jump: Space
  • Dash: Shift
  • Basic attack: J
  • Charged attack: Hold + release K
  • Camera orbit: Right mouse drag
  • Aim control: Hold left click + move mouse (controls character facing + camera)
  • Mouse lock: Click canvas to lock/hide cursor (Esc to release)
  • Dash keybind: Press B, then press any key
  • View mode: V (toggle Classic / Fortnite shoulder camera)
